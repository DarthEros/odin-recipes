Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

This delicious Chicken Tikka Masala can be made by anyone and serves ten! Perfect for those large family gatherings.

Chicken tikka masala is a dish consisting of roasted marinated chicken chunks (chicken tikka) in spiced curry sauce. The curry is usually creamy and orange-coloured. The dish was popularized by cooks from India, Pakistan and Bangladesh living in Britain and is offered at restaurants around the world.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 25g butter
  • 4 onions, roughly chopped
  • 6 tbsp chicken tikka masala paste (shop bought)
  • 2 red peppers, deseeded and cut into chunks
  • 8 boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into cubes
  • 2 x 400g tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 4 tbsp of tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp of mango chutney
  • 150ml double cream
  • 150ml natural yoghurt
  • Chopped coriander leaves, to serve
