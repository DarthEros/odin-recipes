Lasagne Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagne sheets
  • 500ml béchamel sauce
  • 200g grated mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large pan, cook the minced beef over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagne sheets, and then a layer of béchamel sauce. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of béchamel sauce on top.
  5. Sprinkle the grated mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
