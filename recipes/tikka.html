Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken tikka masala is a popular Indian dish that consists of marinated chicken pieces cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce. The chicken is typically marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then grilled or baked until tender. The sauce is made with tomatoes, cream, and a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The dish is often served with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, chili powder, and salt. Add the chicken pieces and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  2. Preheat a grill or oven to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers and grill or bake for 10-15 minutes, until cooked through and slightly charred.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet and bring to a simmer. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and grilled chicken pieces. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes until the sauce is thickened and the chicken is heated through.
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve with rice or naan bread.
