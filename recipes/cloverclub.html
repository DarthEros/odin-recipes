Clover Club

A delicious, raspberry cocktail using gin and vermouth.

Ingredients

  • 40ml gin
  • 15ml dry vermouth
  • 25ml raspberry liqueur
  • 10ml lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp egg white
  • Handful of ice
  • 2 raspberries

Method

  1. Put all of the ingredients into a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously.
  2. Pour through a strainer into a martini glass.
  3. Add one or two raspberries as a garnish.
